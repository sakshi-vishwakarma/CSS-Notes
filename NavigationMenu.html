Mercedes-Benz

The Mercedes-Benz Logo MeaningTogether, the Mercedes-Benz star's 3 points represent the company's drive for universal motorization — but each point also has its individual meaning. The points represent land, sea, and air — environments the company believed they would one day dominate with Mercedes-Benz engines.
